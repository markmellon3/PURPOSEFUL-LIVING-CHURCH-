About the Church

Purposeful Living Church is a Bible-based, family centered, non denominational church with a mission of winning the lost to Christ and teaching the saved to live a purposeful life.

It is the headquarter of Purposeful Living Churches under Kulambiro Christian Ministries (KCM).

Purposeful Living Church is registered by the government of Uganda under Registration no: 11708 to carry out evangelism activities, children welfare and social transformation development activities. The church was founded on May 18th 2014 by Pr. David and Sarah Ssekaboga and from that time the church is steadily growing and impacting the community and the Country at large.

Church Construction Project

Currently our church is under construction and it is our main project as a church. Upon completion, it is estimated to seat approx 1500 people and will house offices as well.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To bring the lost into a relationship with Christ and to equip believers with the wisdom and tools to lead lives defined by divine purpose.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, Spirit-filled community where the promises of God are tangibly realized, and every person's divinely-inspired dreams come to fruition.

Our Core Values

Family

We believe that being part of God's family is the most profound blessing we receive. This sacred bond inspires us to live a life of humility, gratitude, and mutual support.

Faithfulness

We are committed to living a life of steadfast loyalty to God and His Word. We trust in His promise that He is faithful and will always provide a way through every challenge.

Commitment

Our highest commitment is to love the Lord our God with our entire being. This wholehearted love is demonstrated by our resolve to walk according to His statutes.

Teamwork

As the unified Body of Christ, we function as a single team dedicated to serving humanity. We recognize that true strength comes from every member working together in harmony.

The 4 Building Blocks of Purposeful Living

1

Intimate Relationship with God

Purposeful living begins with a vibrant, personal connection with our Creator. This relationship is continually nurtured through fellowship, unconditional love, and unwavering trust.

2

Reflecting the Image of God

Humanity was created to be the visible representation of God on Earth. While this divine image was marred by sin, we are being restored in Christ to fully express His nature and character.

3

Exercising Dominion

We are empowered by God to steward and positively influence the world around us. This dominion is exercised through a life of faith, spiritual authority, and victory over all opposition.

4

Spiritual Reproduction (Discipleship)

A purposeful life is one that actively multiplies its faith by investing in others. We are committed to following the biblical mandate to make and develop mature disciples of Jesus Christ.
